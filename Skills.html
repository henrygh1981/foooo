My UX Skills

User Research

User Experience (UX) Research is the systematic study of target users and their requirements in order to add realistic contexts and insights to design processes. UX researchers adopt various methods to uncover problems and design opportunities and feed actionable findings into the design process.

User Interface Design

User Interface (UI) Design focuses on anticipating what users might need to do and on ensuring that the interface has elements that are delightful, easy to access and understand, and use to facilitate those actions. UI brings together concepts from interaction design, visual design, and information architecture.
